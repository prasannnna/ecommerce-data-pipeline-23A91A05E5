## Dashboard User Guide

## Accessing the Dashboard

### Power BI
- File: dashboards/powerbi/ecommerce_analytics.pbix
- Requires Power BI Desktop (Free)

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary
**Purpose:** Business overview

**KPIs**
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin %

**Visuals**
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Categories
- Payment Method Distribution
- State-wise Sales Map

---

### Page 2: Product Analysis
**Purpose:** Product performance

**Insights**
- Top 10 products drive majority revenue
- Electronics leads profitability

---

### Page 3: Customer Insights
**Purpose:** Customer behavior

**Insights**
- VIP customers contribute major revenue
- Strong repeat purchase behavior

---

### Page 4: Geographic & Trends
**Purpose:** Location & time analysis

**Insights**
- Top 5 states dominate sales
- Weekend sales higher than weekdays

---

## Filters Available
- Date Range
- Category
- State
- Payment Method
